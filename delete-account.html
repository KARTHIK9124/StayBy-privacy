Delete Your Account

You can delete your StayBy account directly from the app or by contacting our support team.

🗑️ Delete Account from the App

The easiest way to delete your account is directly within the StayBy app:

1
Open StayBy and log in Use your registered email and password.
2
Go to Settings Tap the Settings icon from the bottom navigation or the dashboard.
3
Scroll to "Danger Zone" At the bottom of the Settings page, find the "Delete Account" option.
4
Confirm deletion Read the warning and confirm. Your account will be scheduled for deletion within 30 days.

✉️ Request Deletion by Email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us:

  1. Send an email to support.stayby@keysmetrics.in
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your registered email address in the message
  4. We will process your request within 7 business days

📋 What Data Is Deleted or Retained

When your account is deleted, here is what happens to your data:

Data Type Action Retention Period
Account profile (name, email, phone) Deleted Within 30 days
PG details (name, address) Deleted Within 30 days
Rooms and tenant records Deleted Within 30 days
Staff accounts linked to your PG Deleted Within 30 days
Payment and expense records Retained 90 days (for dispute resolution), then deleted
Activity logs Retained 90 days, then deleted

⚠️ Important Notice

  • Account deletion is permanent and irreversible after the 30-day grace period.
  • During the 30-day grace period, you can cancel deletion by contacting support.
  • All active tenant and payment data will be permanently lost.
  • Staff members linked to your PG will also lose access.
  • Any active subscription will not be refunded upon deletion.
