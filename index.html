Privacy Policy

We are committed to safeguarding and preserving your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what happens to any personal information that you provide to us, or that we collect from you.

Last Updated: May 7, 2026

1. Introduction

StayBy ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, disclose, and protect your information when you use our PG management mobile application and related services.

By using the StayBy application, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. This policy applies to all users of the application, including PG owners, managers, and staff.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

a) Account Information

Name, email address, phone number, and password. Passwords are managed securely through AWS Cognito and are never stored in plain text.

b) PG and Property Data

PG name, address, room details, capacity, rent cycle settings, and related property information.

c) Tenant Information

Tenant names, mobile numbers, room assignments, rent amounts, join dates, ID proof submission status, age, gender, occupation, native place, and emergency contact details.

d) Financial Records

Payment amounts, payment dates, payment methods, expense titles, expense amounts, categories, and billing periods.

e) Staff and Access Data

Staff and manager names, email addresses, assigned roles, and permission settings.

f) Usage and Activity Data

Actions performed within the application, activity logs, timestamps, and device information used for crash reporting and analytics.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the StayBy application and its features.
  • To process and record rent payments, expenses, and financial transactions.
  • To send in-app notifications regarding actions performed by staff or managers.
  • To generate reports and analytics to support your PG management operations.
  • To authenticate users and maintain account security.
  • To improve application performance and personalise the user experience.
  • To respond to support requests and resolve technical issues.
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely on Amazon Web Services (AWS) cloud infrastructure. The services we use include:

AWS DynamoDB AWS Cognito AWS Lambda AWS API Gateway

We implement the following security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS / TLS connections.
  • User authentication is managed via secure JSON Web Tokens (JWT).
  • Role-based access control (RBAC) ensures users can only access data relevant to their assigned role.
  • Data is isolated per PG — staff members can only access data belonging to their assigned property.
While we implement all reasonable security precautions, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage all users to use strong, unique passwords and to keep their credentials confidential.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third parties. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Infrastructure Providers: AWS services used to operate the platform, including storage, authentication, and compute services.
  • Legal Compliance: Where required by applicable law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business Transfer: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, data may be transferred to the successor entity with prior notice to users.

We do not share tenant information, payment records, or any personally identifiable data with advertising networks, analytics companies, or any other commercial third parties.

6. Data Access and Role-Based Controls

StayBy enforces strict role-based access control to ensure data is only accessible to authorised users:

Role Access Level
Owner Full access to all data within their PG(s), including staff management, financial reports, and activity logs.
Manager Access to rooms, tenants, and payment records. Cannot manage staff accounts or access all owner-level reports.
Staff Limited access based on specific permissions assigned by the PG owner.

All actions performed by staff and managers are recorded in an activity log that is visible to the PG owner.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Right of Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you at any time.
  • Right to Rectification: You may update or correct inaccurate information through the application settings.
  • Right to Erasure: You may request the deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at stayby.support@gmail.com.
  • Right to Data Portability: You may export your payment and tenant data using the Reports feature within the application.

We will respond to all data rights requests within 30 days of receipt.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account remains active or as necessary to provide our services.

Upon account deletion:

  • Active tenant and payment records will be permanently deleted within 30 days.
  • Encrypted backup copies may be retained for up to 90 days before permanent deletion.
  • Certain data may be retained for a longer period where required by applicable law or regulation.

9. Children's Privacy

StayBy is designed for use by adults aged 18 years and older who operate or manage paying guest accommodations. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18.

If you believe that a minor has provided personal information through our application, please contact us immediately at stayby.support@gmail.com and we will take prompt action to remove such information.

10. Third-Party Services

StayBy integrates with the following third-party services, each of which operates under its own privacy policy:

  • Amazon Web Services (AWS): Cloud infrastructure, data storage, and user authentication.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Anonymous crash reporting used to identify and resolve application errors.
  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymous usage analytics used to understand how the application is used and to improve the user experience.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services for further information on how they handle your data.

  • Update the "Last Updated" date displayed at the top of this page.
  • Notify users of significant changes via an in-app notification.

Your continued use of the StayBy application following the posting of any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
